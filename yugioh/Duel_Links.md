#### Deck Guidelines:
- Main deck contains 20 cards
- No awful No-Cost Staple cards
- Use 2-3 card copies in Main-Deck
- Use 1-2 card copies in Extra-Deck
- Foil order: Glossy,Basic,Prismatic

- Dragon or Behemoth boss monster
- No Limited cards
- No Female cards

#### Secondary Archetype:

- Experimental usage
